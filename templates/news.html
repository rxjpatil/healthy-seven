Blood Pressure Medications Linked to Eczema

A study published in May 2024 reported that certain blood pressure medications, particularly diuretics and calcium channel blockers, might increase the risk of developing eczema, while ACE inhibitors and beta-blockers pose a lower risk​ .

Biking May Reduce the Risk of Knee Pain and Arthritis:

A recent study found that regular biking at any point in life is linked to a lower risk of knee pain and arthritis in the knee joints. This research highlights the potential long-term benefits of incorporating biking into your exercise routine​.
